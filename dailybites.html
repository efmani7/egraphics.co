Brand Strategy | Visual Identity | Packaging Design | Stationery

Dig it! Coffee Co., located in the heart of Las Vegas, goes beyond serving exceptional locally roasted coffee. They're dedicated to providing competitive job opportunities for adults of all abilities, building a community rooted in inclusivity, equality and kindness. Through their coffee expertise and genuine connections, they're redefining workplace inclusivity. Each visit isn't just about the coffee—it's about supporting a movement that values diversity and aims to make a real impact, one sip at a time.

Project Goal: To portray their accessible ethos and fun personality through the power of effective branding, strategy and brand positioning.

“Embeaded is where craft creates connection. The woven thread beneath the wordmark captures the essence of beading—meticulous, expressive, and deeply inclusive. Each curve and color speaks to diverse voices, artfully woven into a unified identity. The bold period seals the statement: complete, confident, and unapologetically creative.”
