PLYR

Apparel Design | Visual Identity


Keep it P!


Player Player (PLYR®) is a Trinidad-based streetwear brand founded in 2016. Known for its bold, urban-inspired fashion, the brand offers a range of apparel including jerseys, tees, hoodies, and customizable pieces. Rooted in creativity, individuality, and quality design, PLYR® blends street style with a distinct Caribbean flair. The brand is active on Instagram, connecting with a diverse audience through fashion drops and lifestyle content.


Project Goal: To build a bold, expressive brand identity that doesn’t just represent PLAYER fashion—it shouts it. A visual statement that blends street culture, individuality, and high-impact design into one unapologetic style movement.

A standout brand design that captures the essence of urban streetwear and elevates PLAYER fashion with a bold, premium finish.

A distinctive brand design that redefines streetwear through a luxurious lens—elevating PLAYER fashion into a statement of style, status, and substance.
